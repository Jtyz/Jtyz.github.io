Tower to the skies

This was made for my Game Development Studios and Level Design and Scripting Studio modules for year 2.

The objectives of the game is to get to the top of the tower.

There are 18 levels in the tower including a tutorial level.

There is also 2 normal non battle scenes.

I was in charge of the level design and most of the models in the game.

This game was made in a team of three.Team name Sea++

This game is made in unity using c#.

You can download the game here!

Controls: WASD movements, CIQRP for controls, inventory, quest, runes and pause. E for interaction and f for tumble. Space for jump.

The last version of the game might contain some bugs that would not allow you to get through to the next stage. Press CHEAT at the same time to teleport.
